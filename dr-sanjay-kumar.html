About Dr. Sanjay Kumar

Source-backed profile references: Harvard Mittal Institute, Harvard Club of India Journal, Economic Times, and impact organizations.

Dr. Sanjay Kumar is a Harvard Kennedy School alumnus and education leader who has spent over two decades at the intersection of global education, public policy, and social impact. He served as India Country Director at Harvard’s Lakshmi Mittal and Family South Asia Institute, leading initiatives across India. His work has also included leadership roles focused on expanding educational access and building impact-led programs. Today, he leads Blue Ocean Education Consulting, helping students craft high-integrity profiles, tell their story with clarity, and execute an admissions strategy aligned with how top universities evaluate applicants.
